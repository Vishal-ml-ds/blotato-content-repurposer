# Vishal Voice Rules

> **The "how Vishal writes" reference.** Applied to every post, DM, offer doc, agent-drafted content.
> **Status:** DRAFT v0.1 — refine after first 20 posts based on what gets engagement and what feels real.

---

## Core principles

1. **Beginner-friendly first.** Vishal is in the beginner-to-architect transition. Talking down to other beginners betrays the journey. Write for the person Vishal was 6 months ago.
2. **Code-first.** Every claim backed by a working example. No abstract framework talk without concrete code.
3. **Daily life analogies.** Technical concepts framed as kitchen / factory / restaurant / traffic analogies. Make it visual, not theoretical.
4. **Numbers over buzzwords.** "Cut response time from 8s to 1.2s" beats "supercharged performance."
5. **Show the failure.** Every win post mentions what was broken before. Authenticity comes from struggle, not polish.

---

## Banned words / phrases

These signal AI-generated or LinkedIn-influencer voice. Never use:

- *Excited / thrilled / delighted to share*
- *Leverage / leveraging*
- *Transformative / game-changing*
- *Synergy / synergize*
- *Best-in-class / world-class*
- *Unlock / unlocked*
- *Revolutionary*
- *Empower / empowering*
- *Cutting-edge*
- *Em-dash overload* (one is fine, three in a sentence is not)
- *Dive deep / let's dive in*

When tempted to use one, ask: "What would I say if I were explaining this to my younger brother?"

---

## Sentence structure

- **Short over long.** If a sentence is over 25 words, break it.
- **Active over passive.** "I shipped X" not "X was shipped by me."
- **First-person Vishal.** Never plural "we" unless an actual team. No royal "we".
- **Concrete over abstract.** "Modal cold-starts hit 12s on first request" beats "performance was suboptimal at scale."

---

## What to write about

See `content-pillars.md` for topic mix. Default: code wins, journey notes, technical breakdowns, career insights.

---

## What NOT to write about

- Politics / culture wars
- Unverified hot takes ("X is dead")
- Generic motivational posts ("Hustle harder!")
- Other people's products without their permission
- NDA-protected content from NBC / Remunance

---

## Format quick rules

- LinkedIn: hook line + breakdown + takeaway. See `formats/linkedin-post-formats.md`.
- DM: 3-4 sentences max. See `formats/dm-templates.md`.
- Twitter (if used): one idea per tweet, thread for breakdowns.
- Long-form (blog/Substack): 800-1500 words, code-heavy, screenshots.

---

## Audit checklist (before publishing)

- [ ] Zero banned words?
- [ ] At least one concrete number or code snippet?
- [ ] Sentences under 25 words?
- [ ] First-person, active voice?
- [ ] Would I say this to my younger brother?
- [ ] Does it match a sample post in `voice/sample-posts/`?
